🌸 About This Website

Kareena Beauty Bot ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website kareenas-beauty-picks-d8i7nfi.gamma.site and associated pages.

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with our website, our Pinterest account (@templateforge04), and our use of the Pinterest API and Amazon Associates Program.

By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the site.

📋 Information We Collect

We keep things simple — we collect as little as possible:

  • No personal data collected — we do not ask for your name, email, phone, or address on this website.
  • No account registration — you can browse freely without creating any account.
  • No forms — this website has no contact forms, surveys, or sign-up forms that collect your details.
  • Automatic data — your browser may send standard technical data (IP address, browser type) to our hosting provider, which we do not access or store ourselves.

📌 Pinterest API Usage

This website uses the Pinterest API to automate the publishing of beauty and skincare pins to our own Pinterest account @templateforge04.

  • We only post content to our own Pinterest account — no one else's.
  • We do not access, read, or store any Pinterest user data, follower data, or engagement data.
  • We do not request permission to view or modify other users' Pinterest accounts.
  • Pinterest's own Privacy Policy applies when you visit our Pinterest profile.
Our Pinterest API usage is strictly limited to posting pins on our own account. We have no access to your Pinterest data whatsoever.

🛒 Amazon Affiliate Links

Kareena's Beauty Picks is a participant in the Amazon Associates Program — an affiliate advertising program that allows us to earn commissions by linking to Amazon.in products.

  • When you click a product link and make a purchase, we earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.
  • Affiliate commissions help us keep this website running and continue discovering the best beauty picks.
  • All product recommendations are genuine — we only feature products we believe are high quality.
  • Amazon may use cookies to track purchases. Please review Amazon's Privacy Notice for details.
✦ Disclosure: As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases. This does not affect our product recommendations.

🍪 Cookies & Tracking

This website itself does not use any tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or advertising cookies.

  • No Google Analytics — we do not use any analytics platform to track visitor behaviour.
  • No ad tracking — we do not run ads on this website and do not use ad tracking pixels.
  • Third-party cookies — Amazon and Pinterest may place their own cookies when you click their links. We have no control over these.

🔒 Data Security

Since we collect virtually no personal data, your privacy risk on our website is minimal. However, we still take reasonable precautions:

  • Our website is served over HTTPS to encrypt data in transit.
  • We do not store any passwords, payment details, or sensitive personal information.
  • No database of users exists — so there is nothing to breach.

Please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. If you have security concerns, please contact us.
